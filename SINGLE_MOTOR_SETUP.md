# Single Motor Setup and Calibration

## Introduction
This document outlines the procedures for setting up and calibrating a single motor in the ALT-Scann8 system. 

## Equipment Needed
- ALT-Scann8 single motor kit
- Power supply
- Calibration tools

## Setup Steps
1. Connect the motor to the power supply.
2. Ensure that all wiring is secure and meets the safety standards.

## Calibration Steps
1. Power on the system and ensure all components are functioning.
2. Follow the calibration procedures outlined in the user manual.
